The Blackpill F303CC is a cheap STM32F303CCT6 development board produced by Robotdyn.
More info can be found on the product page: https://robotdyn.com/stm32f303cct6-256-kb-flash-stm32-arm-cortexr-m4-mini-system-dev-board-3326a9dd-3c19-11e9-910a-901b0ebb3621.html
It has the same form-factor and pinout that the bluepill board.
External 8MHz Crystal used to produce a 72MHz with PLL
CAN bitrate : 1000000
LED on GPIOC pin 13
